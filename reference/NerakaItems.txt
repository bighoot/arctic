NERAKA ENCHANTING

Most of these items suck... but there might be more I'm not aware of...

To get the base items required for enchanting you can either kill the soldiers around the city or just buy them from the dwarf shopkeeper in the eastern part of Neraka. Then you need some extra components:

tail of a rat     - various ways to enter sewers, easiest way is to go to "Southeast Corner" and "enter alley", then "open trapdoor" and go down. 
                    Look for a room down there with NS exits, 'enter tunnel', 'rummage', and hit the rat until its tail pops off.

vial of acid      - on Donny Fugazi in Neraka interior, kill it quick or else it may quaff the vial

coarse black hair - 'yank hair' from Zirko in the Hair of the Troll, southwest area of neraka. Zirko will aggro, can flee

sivak's scale     - need 170 coins, buy a ticket in the arena of death, buy a token inside and challenge sivak. Loot scale from sivak when it dies

dead cockroach from sewers / golden merchant scales from Donny - unknown what to do with these?


When you have the required items, go to the mid-western area of Neraka, find "Wizard's Row" room with N(E)S exits and 'climb stairs'/open door north to Iolanthe. KW to upgrade is 'enhance (item)', each enchant costs 500 coins.
